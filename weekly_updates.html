📘 Paper 1: Design Optimization of Small Capacity SI Engines

  • ✅ Hemispherical chambers improve flame propagation
  • ✅ Retarded exhaust valve timing improves thermal efficiency
  • ✅ Swirl/tumble enhances air-fuel mixing for better combustion

📘 Paper 2: Emission Control in Two-Stroke Engines Using Reed Valves

  • ✅ Reed valves reduce unburnt fuel backflow
  • ✅ Secondary air injection oxidizes HC/CO in exhaust
  • ✅ Emissions reduced by ~30% with simple modifications

Takeaway: Use reed valves and air injection in early designs to meet emission norms with minimal cost.

📘 Paper 3: Thermal Analysis of Cylinder Fins for Enhanced Cooling

  • ✅ Triangular fins outperform rectangular in cooling
  • ✅ Aluminum alloy fins conduct heat better
  • ✅ Fin spacing must balance airflow and surface area

Takeaway: Optimize fin shape and spacing in air-cooled engines for best cooling-to-weight ratio.
